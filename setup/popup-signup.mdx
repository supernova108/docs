## Activating ReferRush Widgets

To display the referral notification pop-up and allow customers to sign up for your program directly, you need to enable the ReferRush app embeds within your Shopify theme customizer.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Step 1: Go to Shopify Dashboard

    Log in to your Shopify admin panel.

  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Step 2: Navigate to Themes

    In the left-hand navigation menu, under "Sales Channels," click on "Online Store." This will automatically open the "Themes" sub-section.

    <img src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/refero1.appspot.com/o/doc-images%2Fsetup%2Fpopup-signup%2Fstep-2.png?alt=media&token=b365814c-45fe-42f4-a78b-7cb276bc03b3" alt="Online Store Themes Menu"/>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Step 3: Customize Your Theme

    Find the live theme you want to add the widgets to (it will usually say "Current theme"). Click the "Customize" button for that theme.

    <img src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/refero1.appspot.com/o/doc-images%2Fsetup%2Fpopup-signup%2Fstep-3.png?alt=media&token=693c0612-d7a4-402c-87b0-9912b288a3bc" alt="Customize Theme Button"/>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Step 4: Access App Embeds

    Inside the theme customizer, look for an icon in the left-hand sidebar that represents "App embeds." It often looks like a puzzle piece. Click on it.

    <img src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/refero1.appspot.com/o/doc-images%2Fsetup%2Fpopup-signup%2Fstep-4.png?alt=media&token=b0cb3f3d-4df3-4856-82f4-6dc2fead9033" alt="App Embeds Section Icon"/>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Step 5: Enable ReferRush Widgets

    In the App Embeds panel, you will see a list of available embeds from your installed apps. Locate the widgets provided by ReferRush, which should be named something like:
    *   **Notification Popup by ReferRush**
    *   **Signup Form Widget by ReferRush**

    Toggle the switch next to *each* of these widgets to the "on" or "enabled" position.

    <img src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/refero1.appspot.com/o/doc-images%2Fsetup%2Fpopup-signup%2Fstep-5.png?alt=media&token=acdc7c6c-cc08-46e9-9f79-fc5a62fa8ec7" alt="Enabling ReferRush Notification Popup and Signup Form Widgets"/>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Step 6: Save Changes

    Crucially, click the "Save" button in the top-right corner of the theme customizer to apply these changes to your live store.
    <img src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/refero1.appspot.com/o/doc-images%2Fsetup%2Fpopup-signup%2Fstep-6.png?alt=media&token=19dd21ea-5741-40a9-84af-5bd39fea5a1a" alt="Save Changes Button"/>
  </Step>
</Steps>

With the widgets enabled, the ReferRush pop-up and sign-up form can now appear on your store. You can further customize their appearance and content within the ReferRush dashboard
