## Installing ReferRush on Shopify

Getting ReferRush up and running on your Shopify store is quick and easy. Follow these steps to connect the app and start building your referral program.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Step 1: Visit ReferRush.com

    Navigate to the official [ReferRush website](https://referrush.com) in your web browser.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Step 2: Click "Get Started"

    Locate and click the "Get Started" button on the ReferRush homepage. This action will redirect you to the ReferRush app listing page within the Shopify App Store.

    <Info>
      You will be taken directly to the Shopify App Store page for ReferRush.
    </Info>
    <img src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/refero1.appspot.com/o/doc-images%2Fgetting-started%2Finstallation%2Fstep-1.png?alt=media&token=f6126102-9b22-4588-9db0-9f578bccb4a3" alt="Click Get Started Button"/>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Step 3: Initiate Installation from Shopify App Store

    On the ReferRush app page in the Shopify App Store, click the "Install" or "Add app" button. Shopify will then redirect you to the installation confirmation screen within your Shopify admin panel.
    <img src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/refero1.appspot.com/o/doc-images%2Fgetting-started%2Finstallation%2Fstep-2.png?alt=media&token=cbe52246-a4be-4f7e-9514-db8e78b5f700" alt="Shopify App Store Install Button"/>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Step 4: Confirm Installation in Shopify Admin

    Review the permissions requested by the ReferRush app. Once you're ready, click the "Install app" button on this screen within your Shopify admin.
    <img src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/refero1.appspot.com/o/doc-images%2Fgetting-started%2Finstallation%2Fstep-3.png?alt=media&token=c7648929-8fe3-446d-a611-569b529d46b1" alt="Shopify Admin Install Confirmation"/>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Installation Complete!

    Congratulations! ReferRush is now installed on your Shopify store. You should be redirected to the ReferRush dashboard within your Shopify admin, where you can begin setting up your first referral program.
  </Step>
</Steps>
You're now ready to configure your [Pricing](/getting-started/pricing)!
